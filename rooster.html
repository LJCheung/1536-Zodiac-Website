Rooster

The main characteristics of a Rooster are:

  • Foresighted
  • Ambitious
  • Meticulous
  • Independent

Strengths of a Rooster

They are deep thinkers considered to be honest, bright, communicative, ambitious, capable and warm-hearted. Strong self-respect and seldom relying on others are their basic characteristics. As most of them are born pretty or handsome, they prefer to dress up. They have a quick mind and hot temper. They like to be busy and neat. With strong enterprise and conscientiousness, they are reluctant to fall behind others and hate dawdlers. They always complete things according to their previous plans. Well-organized mind makes them more efficient. During work, they can always put forward distinctive ideas and solutions about the development of the company. Thus, they have enviable promotion prospect in their early years.

Weaknesses of a Rooster

Those born in the Year of the Rooster might be enthusiastic about something quickly, but soon be impassive. They are often a bit eccentric and have difficulty in relating with others. They are critical and think they are always right, so generally speaking, they are unwilling to accept other peopleâ€™s suggestions but like to lecture others. Like their fortunes, their emotions swing from very high very slow in a short time. They can be positive but selfish, caustic, too outspoken, and some narrow-minded. If they can overcome their arrogance and be sincere in speaking, they will make great progress.
